I am a postdoctoral researcher in Natural Language Processing at EPFL, specializing in trustworthy AI, multilingual large language models, and comprehensive model evaluation.

My research focuses on benchmarking, stereotype and bias measurement, safety analysis, and evaluation of large language model capabilities in real-world contexts (e.g., education, STEM reasoning, multilingual environments).

I collaborate closely with interdisciplinary teams across computer science, education, linguistics, and the social sciences to design, implement, and evaluate models that address real-world challenges.

Through this work, my goal is to advance the development of safe, transparent, and globally accessible AI systems.

---

## Research Interests

- **Trustworthy AI and LLM evaluation**  
- **Bias, stereotypes, and fairness in language models**  
- **Human-in-the-loop evaluation**  
- **AI for education**
